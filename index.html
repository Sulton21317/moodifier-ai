About Moodifier AI

Moodifier AI is an innovative Android application designed to help you understand and manage your emotional state. Using advanced AI technology, we analyze your feelings and provide personalized insights, motivation, and support.

Whether you're feeling hyped, angry, sad, or unsure, Moodifier AI offers tailored responses and audio feedback to help you navigate your emotions. Track your mood history, discover patterns, and gain valuable insights into your emotional wellness journey.

Built with modern Android technologies including Jetpack Compose, Room Database, and Hilt for a smooth, reliable experience. Our mission is to make emotional wellness accessible and engaging for everyone.
